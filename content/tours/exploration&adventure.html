EXPLORATION & ADVENTURE

An unforgettable 22-day adventure to explore the diverse landscapes of Madagascar, from the lush rainforests of Andasibe, the iconic Baobab Alley, the breathtaking Tsingy de Bemaraha, to the pristine beaches of the southwest coast. This journey offers an immersive experience through wildlife, unique rock formations, cultural encounters, and stunning coastal scenery.

Itinerary for this 22-days

Antananarivo - Andasibe - Antsirabe - Miandrivazo - Morondava - Bekopaka - Belo sur mere - Ambatomilo - Salary - Ifaty - Tulear - Ranohira - Fianaratsoa - Ranomafana - Ambositra - Antananarivo

ITINERARY

DAY 1 : ANTANANARIVO

Arrival at Ivato. Steps for the visa. Guests are welcomed by our representative and transfer to hotel. Installation at the hotel. Overnight at hotel.

DAY 2 : ANTANANARIVO - ANDASIBE

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure to Andasibe. On the road, enjoy a panoramic view of the eastern highlands of Madagascar. Stop in Marozevo to visit the private reserve of Peyreiras. During the visit, we can observe endemic island animals such as chameleons, frogs, and more. After a 1-hour visit, continue towards Moramanga. There is a possibility to visit the market and explore the city of Moramanga. Then, continue the road to Andasibe National Park. Arrival in the late afternoon. Accommodation at the hotel.

DAY 3 : ANDASIBE

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure to visit Analamazaotra National Park, the only national park where you can find the largest lemur in Madagascar, the Indri Indri. The park is located in a humid area, home to a variety of flora and fauna, many of which are endemic. We will have a 2-3 hour visit. In the afternoon, visit the Vakona Reserve, with the possibility of seeing other species of lemurs and various animals. Accommodation at the hotel.

DAY 4 : ANDASIBE - ANTSIRABE

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure to Antsirabe. On the way, enjoy a panoramic view of the rice fields and the tombs of the Highlands of Madagascar. Stop for a visit in Ambatolampy to see the manufacture of aluminum pots. In the afternoon, arrival in Antsirabe. Visit the City of Waters and some Malagasy craft workshops. Accommodation at the hotel.

DAY 5 : ANTSIRABE - MORONDAVA

Breakfast at hotel Leaving the hotel in the morning for joining Morondava. View of the first baobab alley in the west of Madagascar and typical landscape with rice paddies. Arrival in Morondava, visit of the city and the beach. Overnight at hotel

DAY 6 : MORONDAVA - BEKOPAKA

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure early in the morning for joining Bekopaka, in dirt track road. Transfer by ferry in Belo sur Tsiribihina. Then continuation towards Bekopaka. Late in the afternoon, arrival at the hotel Overnight at hotel

DAY 7 : BEKOPAKA

Breakfast at the hotel. Day dedicated to the visit of the National Park Tsingy of Bemaraha. It is important to note that this park is ranked among the heritages of the UNESCO World Heritage Site. In the morning, we make a visit at least 4 hours to see and admire the big Tsingy. In the afternoon, a visit to 1 to 1h30 for discovering the small Tsingy. Overnight at hotel

DAY 8 : BEKOPAKA - MORONDAVA

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure early in the morning to return to Belo sur Tsiribihina. Then transfer by ferry to Tsimafana. Then dirt track till Morondava. Sunset in the famous baobab alley. Arrival in Morondava at the end of the afternoon. Overnight at hotel

DAY 9 : MORONDAVA - BELO SUR MER

Breakfast at the hotel. We continue our adventure on the dirty road with an unforgettable landscape. Arrival in Belo sur Mer, visit of the village. Possibility of spending time at the beach Belo sur Mer Overnight at hotel

DAY 10 : BELO SUR MER - MOROMBE

Day to discover on the way the southwest part of the island. Pass by the Mangoky River to the town of Bevoay. On the way, we will see a magnificent landscape with the baobabs. In the afternoon, we will arrive at the Mozambique channel, Morombe beach. Accommodation at the hotel

DAY 11 : MOROMBE - SALARY

After the breakfast, we will continue our adventure on the coastal track of zebu carts. We will passe through a thorny jungle where we will find many endemic trees of the island. Possibility also to admire the baobab alley of Madagascar. Accommodation at the hotel

DAY 12 : SALARY

Breakfast at the hotel. Day off Accommodation at the hotel

DAY 13 : SALARY - IFATY

Breakfast at the hotel. We continue our adventure through the sand of the southern coast of Madagascar. We will see during the trip the Mozambique channel with villages of fishermen. Accommodation at the hotel

DAY 14 : IFATY

Breakfast at the hotel. Day off Overnight at hotel

DAY 15 : IFATY - TULEAR - RANOHIRA

Breakfast at the hotel. Exit to leave the southern part of the big island during this baobab tour. On the road, admiration of other varieties of baobab alley and the arid landscape of the south with the famous Mahafaly tombs. Overnight at hotel

DAY 16 : RANOHIRA

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure by car to the National Park of Isalo. A day dedicated for visiting the fauna and flora of the park, and also the natural pool, the black and blue pool. The park is known for its Canyon also. In the afternoon, sunset in the famous ``window of Isalo``. Overnight at hotel

DAY 17 : RANOHIRA - FIANARANTSOA

Breakfast at the hotel. Leaving Ranohira early in the morning to follow up this baobab tour. In Ambalavao visit of Anja reserve where we can admire the famous lemur Catta Maki emblem of Madagascar. Then we visit the papermaking workshop Antemoro in Ambalavao and weaving silk. We arrived at late afternoon in the capital of Betsileo. Overnight at hotel

DAY 18 : FIANARANTSOA - RANOMAFANA

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure to the village of Ranomafana. Visit of the National Park of Ranomafana. A visit of 3 to 4 hours to admire the lemurs, several faunas and floras endemic to this part of Madagascar. In the afternoon, visit the village. Possibility to swim in the thermal water of Ranomafana. Overnight at hotel

DAY 19 : RANOMAFANA - ANTSIRABE

Breakfast at the hotel. Departure to Antsirabe. Along the way, we can admire the scenery with the typical architecture of Betsileo ethnic and rice fields. Arrival in Ambositra, visit of some craft stores. Ambositra is the capital of the Malagasy handicrafts and wood carving. After we will continue the journey till Antsirabe, possibility to visit the Avenue of Antsirabe in rickshaw (pousse-pousse). Overnight at hotel

DAY 20 : ANTSIRABE - ANTANANARIVO

Breakfast at the hotel. Road back to the capital of Madagascar after this long baobab tour. Arrived in Antananarivo visit of the old city and the center. Overnight at hotel

DAY 21 : ANTANANARIVO - FLIGHT BACK

Breakfast at the hotel. Visit the craft market, the largest market in the capital. Transfer to Ivato airport for your international flight. Boarding formalities
